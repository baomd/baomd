About Dr. Nguyen

Dermatologist, Educator, Researcher, and Advocate for Healthier Skin

Commitment to Excellence

Dr. Quoc-Bao Nguyen is a double board-certified dermatologist and fellowship-trained Mohs micrographic and reconstructive surgeon specializing in skin cancer treatment, reconstructive surgery, medical dermatology, and cosmetic dermatology. Born and raised in Austin, Texas, he is honored to return to his hometown to provide compassionate, high-quality, evidence-based care to the community he loves.

Dr. Quoc-Bao Nguyen currently serves as President of the Austin Dermatological Society.

Dr. Nguyen's academic journey is defined by excellence. He graduated Summa Cum Laude from The University of Texas at Austin, earning a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry through the prestigious Dean's Scholars Honors Program, completing Special Honors, and finishing at the top of his class with a 4.0 GPA.

He received his Medical Degree with Honors from Texas A&M University, where he was inducted into the Alpha Omega Alpha (AOA) Honor Medical Society in recognition of his outstanding academic performance and leadership.

He completed his intern year in Neurosurgery at The University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston, followed by a dedicated clinical research year, where he served as a sub-investigator in approximately 20 dermatology clinical research trials. During this time, he also earned an MBA in Finance from The University of Houston, expanding his ability to collaborate effectively with pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies to advance innovative dermatologic therapies.

He completed his Dermatology Residency at the prestigious MD Anderson Cancer Center and The University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston, specializing in skin cancer management. He further honed his expertise by completing a Mohs Micrographic Surgery and Dermatologic Oncology Fellowship at Geisinger Medical Center in Pennsylvania, focusing on complex facial reconstruction.

Comprehensive Aesthetic Care

In addition to his surgical expertise, Dr. Nguyen is passionate about helping his patients look refreshed and feel confident in their skin. He offers a full spectrum of cosmetic treatments—including Botox®, Dysport®, dermal fillers, microneedling, platelet-rich plasma (PRP), chemical peels, laser resurfacing, scar revision, and cosmetic surgery—as well as advanced procedures for cellulite reduction, body contouring, skin tightening, sclerotherapy, and hair restoration.

Academic Contributions

Throughout his academic and professional career, Dr. Nguyen has received numerous honors, including the ASDS Resident Scholarship and the World Congress of Dermatology Scholarship. With more than 30 publications in leading dermatology journals and over 100 presentations at state, national, and international conferences, he has made meaningful contributions to dermatologic science.

Approach to Patients

When I meet a patient, I focus on more than the skin—I focus on the person. Every individual brings their own concerns, goals, and experiences, and I believe effective care begins with truly listening.

Education is central to my philosophy. When patients understand their diagnosis and the reasoning behind treatment recommendations, they feel more confident and engaged in their care.

My practice blends evidence-based dermatology with a personalized approach. I stay current with the latest surgical, medical, and cosmetic advancements, but I also recognize that the best treatment is the one tailored to your unique skin, goals, and comfort level.
